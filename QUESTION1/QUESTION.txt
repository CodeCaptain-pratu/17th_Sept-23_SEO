QUESTION:Q1. What is SEO and explain the importance of SEO?
Answer:
1) SEO stands for search engine optimization
2) It helps you optimize the website so that websites have better 
ranking on search engines like Google,Bing and Yahoo.
3) The ultimate goal of SEO is to rank higher in search engine results
pages for related keyword and phrases, which can increase traffic 
and ultimately revenue for the website.

The importance of SEO : 
1) Meta tags are imporatant for SEO because they provide information 
to search engines about the content on your website
2) Meta tags helps to improve the websites visiblity and ranking in 
search engine results pages.
